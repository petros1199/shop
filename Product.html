The ASUS 15.6" Republic of Gamers STRIX G15 Gaming laptop is built with hardware and aesthetics aimed for the gaming crowd. Thanks to its 2.6 GHz Intel Core i7-10750H six-core processor, 16GB of DDR4 RAM, and NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2070 graphics card, it can easily power through everyday tasks and even play graphically demanding games on its 240 Hz Full HD display. Moreover, a 1TB NVMe PCIe M.2 SSD improves everything from boot-up times to loading screens. Connectivity options include USB Type-A and Type-C ports, HDMI, Wi-Fi 6, Gigabit LAN, and Bluetooth 5.0. Other integrated features include speakers, microphones, a backlit keyboard, and a combo audio jack. The operating system installed is Windows 10 Home.
